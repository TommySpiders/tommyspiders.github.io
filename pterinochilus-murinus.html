Pterinochilus murinus

The Orange Baboon Tarantula (OBT)

Basic Information

Scientific name: Pterinochilus murinus

Common name: Orange Baboon Tarantula

Native to: Central & Southern Africa

Care Sheet

Temperature: 24–28°C

Humidity: 50–70%

Enclosure: Dry substrate, lots of anchor points for webbing.

Behaviour: Highly defensive, fast, very heavy webber.

Growth rate: Fast

Temperament

This species is known as the “Orange Bitey Thing.” Not recommended for beginners — extremely defensive and quick.
